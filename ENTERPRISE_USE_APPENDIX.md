# Enterprise Use Appendix

## Purpose
This appendix defines acceptable enterprise deployment boundaries.

## Approved Enterprise Uses
- Internal AI governance tooling
- Compliance review frameworks
- Ethics audit systems
- Dataset evaluation pipelines

## Mandatory Constraints
- No individual profiling
- No predictive analytics
- Canon adherence required
- Internal-only access

## Audit Rights
Hollow House Institute retains audit and review rights for licensed enterprise deployments.

## Termination Clause
Violation of Canon or License results in immediate termination of rights.
